Newsbuzzwords.com continuously scans a large group of news websites around the world in search for trending words. The identified words are visible for 24 hours. The quoted times are CEST and represent the initial identification time of a buzzword. All words that are displayed met the initial 'trending' requirements. Buzzing strength, varying from 1 to 4 green circles, shows the trending degree of each word.
